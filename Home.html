STRIDE IDENTITY

Our Mission

The mission of STRIDE SACCO shall be “To create trustworthy avenues for saving and borrowing at low interest and empower members in financial management skills for community development.

Values

  1. Integrity
  2. Professionalism
  3. Transparency
  4. Accountability

Vision

  1. To create “Healthy financially empowered and Prosperous members for development
  2. To be the leading SACCO providing quality and dynamic financial services to our members
  3. A leading SACCO for empowering members for financial independence for development

Business Mission

  1. To enlighten staff in financial management
  2. Provide trustworthy avenues for saving and borrowing at low interest
  3. Empowering staff with financial management skills for personal development with superior management skills,
    professionalism, integrity, and personal involvement in all projects
